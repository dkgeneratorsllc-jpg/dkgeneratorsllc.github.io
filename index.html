About DK Generators & Mechanical Services

DK Generators & Mechanical Services was founded to provide reliable generator, HVAC, refrigeration, and mechanical service throughout Huntingdon County, the Raystown Lake Region, and Central Pennsylvania.

Owner Dustin Kanagy brings over 20 years of hands-on experience working on power generation, electrical, HVAC, refrigeration, and mechanical systems.

Training includes the Pennsylvania College of Technology Power Generation Program and the Huntingdon County Career and Technology Center Electrical Occupations Program under Robert Prough.

Experience includes work on mobile and stationary generators, correctional facilities, VA hospitals, 911 communications sites, commercial facilities, HVAC systems, refrigeration equipment, site lighting, kitchen equipment, and power generation systems ranging from fractional kilowatt units to 2-megawatt generators.
